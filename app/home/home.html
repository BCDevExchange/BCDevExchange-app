The British Columbia Developers' Exchange (BCDevX) mission is to enhance collaboration between Industry, Broader Public Sector, and Government, to allow for the sharing of digital resources and knowledge, and to help stimulate innovation and technology growth in BC.

The development of this project is continuing to evolve. Please visit our blog to get updates on our progress.

Get Involved
