BCDevX




The British Columbia Developer Exchange (BCDevX) is intended to enhance collaboration between Government, Broader Public Sector, and Industry, to allow for the sharing of digital resources and knowledge, and to help stimulate innovation and technology growth in BC.

The development of this project is continuing to evolve. Please visit our blog to get updates on our progress.
