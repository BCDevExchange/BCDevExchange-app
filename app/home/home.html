The mission of the British Columbia Developers' Exchange (BCDevX) is to enhance collaboration between Industry, Broader Public Sector, and Government, to allow for the sharing of digital resources and knowledge, and to help stimulate innovation and technology growth in BC.

The development of this project is continuing to evolve. Please visit our blog to get updates on our progress.

A project from the B.C. Government

B.C. Government is working with industry and BPS to co-design and co-create BCDevX, while demonstrating itself as an early adopter.

Let's Talk

We're trying to build the community and get feedback on what we're doing. We'd love to hear your ideas and suggestions.

Check out our blog to find out what the BCDevX team is up to.
