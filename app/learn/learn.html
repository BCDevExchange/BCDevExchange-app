What is BCDevX?

The British Columbia Developer Exchange (BCDevX) is intended to enhance collaboration between Government, Broader Public Sector, and Industry, to allow for the sharing of digital resources and knowledge, and to help stimulate innovation and technology growth in B.C. Although the B.C. Government is spearheading this project, the goal is bring other members of the community on board, to help provide direction and establish governance.

The development of this project is continuing to evolve. Please check back regularly for updates, or sign up for Notifications.
