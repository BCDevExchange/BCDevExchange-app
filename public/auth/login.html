Join the BCDevExchange experiment!

Why Sign Up?

Sign up with the BCDevExchange experiment:

  • Be the first to learn about new developments and have input into its direction
  • Get tapped into the community, and gain early access to Discovery initiatives
  • Start building your reputation with the community, and be ready for Contract Opportunities as they become available
  • Take advantage of Single Sign on with associated accounts

Here's How!

You can sign up for the BCDevExchange experiment with either a Github account or a LinkedIn account.

After creating an account using one of these options, you can associate the other one as well. For example, after signing up with a GitHub account, you can tie in your LinkedIn account at a later time (and vice versa).

| Sign in with GitHub

  • Point people to your repos
  • Share GitHub resources
  • Make it easier for people to see what you've been doing in GitHub

| Sign in with LinkedIn

  • Show off your skills, experience and credentials
  • Make it easier for people to find and contact you about opportunities
